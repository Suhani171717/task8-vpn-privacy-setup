 Task 8: Working and Understanding VPN

 Objective
Understand the role of VPNs in protecting privacy and enabling secure communication.

 Tools Used
- ProtonVPN (Free Tier)
- Web Browser (Google Chrome)
- whatismyipaddress.com



 Step-by-Step Execution

  1. **VPN Account Setup**
- Visited [https://protonvpn.com](https://protonvpn.com)
- Created a free account using email ID.
- Confirmed account via email and logged in.

  ![Signup](./screenshots/vpn_signup.png)



 2. **Download and Installation**
- Downloaded the ProtonVPN client for Windows.
- Installed the VPN and completed setup.

  ![Installation](./screenshots/vpn_installation.png)



 3. **Connected to a VPN Server**
- Logged in to ProtonVPN client.
- Selected a **free Netherlands server** and connected.
- Verified the VPN status.

  ![Connected](./screenshots/vpn_connected.png)


 4. **Verified IP Address Change**
- Before connecting: Local IP and location displayed.
- After connecting: IP changed to a Netherlands-based IP.
- Used [https://whatismyipaddress.com](https://whatismyipaddress.com) for verification.

  ![IP Changed](./screenshots/ip_changed.png)



 5. **Secure Browsing**
- Accessed websites like `example.com` and Google while VPN was ON.
- Pages loaded securely, confirming encrypted tunnel was active.

  ![Secure Browsing](./screenshots/secure_browsing.png)



 6. **Disconnected VPN & Compared**
- Disconnected ProtonVPN.
- Observed original IP and location restored.
- Compared browsing speed before and after VPN usage.

  ![Disconnected](./screenshots/vpn_disconnected.png)



 7. **VPN Research Summary**
- **What is a VPN?** A VPN encrypts your internet connection and hides your IP.
- **Protocols Used:** OpenVPN, IKEv2/IPSec, WireGuard.
- **Advantages:** Security, privacy, bypass censorship, avoid tracking.
- **Limitations:** Slight drop in speed, doesn't prevent malware, no absolute anonymity.


Conclusion:This task helped me gain practical experience in VPN usage. I understood how VPNs enhance privacy by hiding IP addresses and encrypting traffic, while also exploring their real-world limitations.
